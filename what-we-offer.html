Work with us

Our process works to provide insureds with peace of mind, knowing workers' compensation is expertly managed by a team focused on their success.

1

Initial Assessment

We thoroughly analyze the company’s industry,
history, and logistical details 

2

Customized Plan Development

We build a customized workers’ comp insurance plan
to address specific risks and requirements and ensure
optimal protection for the workforce

3

Risk Management Strategies

We implement proactive strategies to minimize
potential hazards, maintain a safer work environment
and lower workers’ comp rates

4

Claims Handling and Support

In the event of filing a workers’ comp claim, we ensure a
smooth and efficient process, managing all aspects of
claims handling, from initial reporting to resolution,
providing continuous support and clear communication to all
parties involved

5

Continuous Monitoring and Adjustments

Your broker will continuously monitor your policy
to keep coverage up-to-date with any changes in
business operation or industry regulations

6

Regular Reviews and Updates

We conduct regular reviews of workers comp
insurance plans so we can identify opportunities.
